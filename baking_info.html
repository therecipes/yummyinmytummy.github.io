Blueberry pie is a pie with a blueberry filling. It is considered
one of the easiest pies to make. It usually has a top and bottom crust.
The top crust can be a circular crust but the pie can also have a
crumble crust or no top crust at all. Blueberry Pies are often eaten in
the summertime because that is when blueberries are in season.
